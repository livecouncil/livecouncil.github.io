What

  • LIVECouncil is a digital hub for Professionals seeking to connect with Peers who are experts or have had success or experience in similar Roles or with specific Products or Tasks

Who

  • LIVECouncil members are employees of global corporations, small and medium size companies, Non-Profit organizations, Research Institutions as well as members of Academia.

Which

  • LIVECouncil maintains a growing list of trending topics in the Industry that professionals (our members) are tackling everyday.
  • LIVECouncil members can set up their profiles to have dual roles, be the Expert that peers seek out to deliver a session in their areas of expertise or be a Learner who is looking to book sessions with other Experts.

How

  • An intuitive search & match engine provides recommendations of Expert LIVECouncil members based on details that Learners are seeking, based on their profiles and topic criteria.
  • LIVECouncil sessions are audio-only and are booked as 20 minute phone calls enabled by the LIVECouncil platform.
